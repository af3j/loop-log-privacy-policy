Information We Collect

Loop Log is designed to be local-only.

Loop Log does not collect, upload, sell, share, or transmit your thought entries, notes, themes, distress ratings, compulsion urges, responses, or history to us or to any server.

The information you enter into Loop Log is stored locally on your device.

Information Stored on Your Device

Loop Log may store the following information locally on your device:

Intrusive thought entries
Thought themes
Distress ratings
Compulsion urge ratings
Compulsion response information
Time-stuck selections
Optional notes
App settings
Disclaimer acknowledgement
Export-related data created by you

No Account Required

Loop Log does not require an account. You do not need to provide your name, email address, phone number, or login information to use the app.

No Ads or Analytics

Loop Log does not use advertising SDKs. Loop Log does not use analytics SDKs. Loop Log does not track your activity across other apps or websites.

No Cloud Sync

Loop Log does not provide cloud sync in this version. Your entries are not uploaded to a cloud server by Loop Log.

Internet Access

Loop Log is intended to work without internet access. If the app does not request internet permission, it cannot send your thought log data over the internet.

Data Sharing

Loop Log does not share your data with third parties. Loop Log does not sell your data. Loop Log does not transfer your thought entries, notes, or tracking history to advertisers, analytics providers, data brokers, or other outside parties.

CSV Export

Loop Log may allow you to export your entries to a CSV file.

Exporting data is controlled by you. Once you export a file, you are responsible for where that file is saved, stored, shared, backed up, or deleted.

Please be careful when sharing exported files, because they may contain sensitive personal information.

Data Deletion

You can delete individual entries in the app if this feature is available.

You can delete all locally stored app data by using your Android device settings to clear the app’s storage or by uninstalling the app.

If you uninstall Loop Log, clear app storage, factory reset your device, or replace your device, your locally stored entries may be lost unless you have exported or backed them up.

App Updates

Normal app updates from Google Play should not delete your local Loop Log entries. However, you should export important data before making major device changes, clearing storage, uninstalling the app, or replacing your phone.

Backups

Loop Log does not operate its own cloud backup service.

Depending on your Android device settings, your device or Google account may provide separate backup features. Those backups are controlled by Android, Google, your device manufacturer, or your selected backup provider, not by Loop Log.

Security

Loop Log stores data locally on your device. You are responsible for protecting access to your device using your device’s available security features, such as a screen lock, PIN, password, fingerprint, or face unlock.

No method of electronic storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children’s Privacy

Loop Log is not intended for children. Loop Log is intended for use by adults who want a private self-tracking tool for intrusive thoughts and related patterns.

Health and Medical Disclaimer

Loop Log is a private self-tracking tool.

Loop Log is not a medical device, diagnosis tool, treatment tool, therapy service, crisis service, or substitute for professional mental-health care.

Loop Log does not diagnose OCD or any other condition. Loop Log does not provide medical advice.

If you have questions about your mental health, please speak with a qualified mental-health professional.

If you feel at risk of harming yourself or someone else, contact emergency services or a crisis hotline immediately.
